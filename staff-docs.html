Moderation Procedures

1
Warning System

Give verbal warnings first, then written warnings logged directly in melonly.

If not inside of melonly, kindly ask the owner for an invite.

2
Kick/Ban Protocol

Always discuss with a senior staff member before issuing bans longer than 24 hours

3
Evidence Collection

Take screenshots/videos of rule violations before taking action

4
Appeal Process

Direct appeals to #ban-appeals channel, senior staff will handle
