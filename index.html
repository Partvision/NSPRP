Server Rules

1. Respect All Members

Treat all players and staff with respect. No harassment, bullying, or discrimination will be tolerated.

2. Roleplay Realistically

Maintain character and adhere to realistic scenarios. No fail RP, powergaming, or unrealistic actions.

3. Follow Chain of Command

Respect the hierarchy within your department and the server staff. Follow lawful orders from superiors.

4. No Cheating or Exploiting

Any form of cheating, glitching, or exploiting bugs is strictly forbidden and will result in a permanent ban.

5. Use Common Sense

If something seems like it would be against the rules, it probably is. Play fair and contribute to a positive environment.

6. New Life Rule (NLR)

If your character is killed, you must forget the events leading to your death. Do not return to the same RP scenario.

7. STS Protocol

  • Once STS is called, all participants must remain silent (voice, in-game text, etc.) unless directly addressed by staff.
  • Line up shoulder-to-shoulder in a single, organized line. Do not stand behind others or form multiple rows.
  • Respond exactly as directed by staff. If asked to "jump once to confirm," do only that — nothing more, nothing less.

8. Misconduct & Exploits

  • Disrespectful behavior, hate speech, harassment, or toxic attitudes will result in disciplinary action.
  • Trolling, minging, mic spamming, or intentionally disrupting sessions will lead to immediate removal.
  • Always follow staff instructions without argument; non-compliance will result in removal or punishment.
  • Using exploits, glitches, or abusing mechanics is a permanent-ban offense. Report bugs instead.
  • All actions must align with proper roleplay standards—act realistically according to your role.

Northstone Point Police Dept.
